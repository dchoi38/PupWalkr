About PupWalkr

The Walkrs

Only fully qualified dog walkers are assigned to your pup. All of our walkrs are experienced animal handlers and meet or exceed the qualifications below. We are very selective in our screening process in order to deliver and maintain the highest quality service to our customers.

Two Interviews

All Walkrs must pass a phone screen and an in-person interview/orientation. We look for passionate people with past dog handling experience.

University Education

Our Walkrs are attending, or have graduated from, a university.

Insured & Bonded

All pups and Walkrs are fully covered under our insurance policy for the entire duration of the walk.

Animal Experience

All Walkrs have prior experience in handling dogs. These can include dog walking, dog sitting, owning a dog, or prior employment at a dog shelter or doggy daycare.

Background Check

All Walkrs pass a FCRA compliant background check as part of the screening process. 

True Dog People

We know how much your pup means to you--that's why so much of our resources go towards finding the best Walkrs.

PupWalkr Insurance

All Walkrs are fully bonded and insured under our comprehensive insurance coverage.

Property Damage

Damage to property owned by you or others.

Vet Care

If medical attention is required by your pup as a result of our service.

Lost Keys

If keys are lost or damaged by our Walkrs, we will cover the cost of new locks.

Medical Expenses

Any persons harmed by your pup on the walk.

The Founders

PupWalkr was founded in late 2014 by two Atlanta natives. Co-Founders Alex and David ran into problems while working full-time jobs that were decidedly not the "nine to five" while taking care of two lovely greyhounds (Wolfman and Kerry). They began to wonder why there wasn't a simple, modern way to request a dog walker and prevent accidents in their apartment. From this, the idea for a technology driven, on-demand dog walking service was born.

1. Who's walking my dog?

Only fully qualified Walkrs will be assigned to your pup. We only partner with Walkrs that meet all of our qualifications, and are very selective with those we bring on board. All of our Walkrs are experienced animal handlers and meet or exceed the qualifications for animal handling, have graduated from (or are attending) a university, have passed a backround check, and gone through our interview process.

We understand how important your pup is to you, so we do all we can to ensure that the Walkrs will be up to your standards.

There is more detailed information about our Walkrs above!

2. Will I always have the same Walkr?

We can't guarantee that you will always have the same Walkr. Our Walkrs have the freedom to set their own schedules as well, so they won't always be online. But, this system is what allows us to bring PupWalkr to you at such an affordable price!

We understand that it can be scary letting people you don't know into your home to care for your pup, and that's why we go through so much to vet every Walkr we partner with (2 interviews, background checks, training walks).

Also, we do our best to assign Walkrs to a certain area, so while you most likely won't have the same Walkr every time, you will have the same group of Walkrs helping you out!

3. How does the dog walker access my apartment?

For your Door:
- Hide your key, and let us know of its super secret location via email (support@pupwalkr.com) or text (404-509-6292)
- Put a copy of your key in a lockbox
- Email us (support@pupwalkr.com) with another method you prefer to use
- For apartment residents, you can also approve a key release for PupWalkr through us or directly with your front office

For your Gate:
- Approve a key release for PupWalkr through us or directly with your front office
- Give us your gate code call box #
- Give us a key fob (by hiding it, storing it in an accessible lockbox, or giving us a copy to put in a lockbox on your property)
- Choose another method that you'd prefer

Please note that our Walkr's will not open any doors once inside the residence, unless it is required to get the pup.

4. How much does a dog walk cost? What about the Daycare?

Each dog walk will decrease in cost based on the walk package you select. You have the option of buying a Single ($18/walk), a bundle of 5 ($15/walk), a bundle of 10 ($13/walk), or a bundle of 20 ($11/walk).

The same discounts are also applied to the Daycare. You can buy them one at a time (Singles: $30/day) or in a bundle of 3 ($28/day). Save yourself the trips to/from daycare, while your pup rest in the comfort of their home.

The bundles let you purchase your walks in bulk ahead of time and save money while doing so. They will never expire and will always stay with your account.

The bundles are especially great if you know your schedule (e.g. you need your dog walked every weekday around 1:00pm or you will be travelling for 3 days out of a week). You can set all of your needs at once and know that your pup will be taken care of! Set and forget.



5. What can I do with the walks once purchased? Can I cancel/reschedule?

The walks you purchase will never expire. You always have the flexibility to schedule, cancel, or reschedule a walk up to 1 hour prior to the start time.

We maintain this policy to give you as much flexibility as possible and to accommodate you and your schedule.


6. What is the PupWalkr Daycare? What does it include?

We currently offer an all inclusive full-day care package covering all of your pup's daily needs through 4 (10-15 minute) visits over 12 hours. Each visit can include potty breaks, feeding, water, medication (if needed), and any additional needs your pup may have.

This saves you the trips to/from daycare, while also letting your pup rest in the comfort of their home.

For your first daycare, please email us with the following information:
1. The time(s) you'd like your pup(s) walked
2. The time(s) you'd like your pup(s) fed
3. How much food per feeding
4. Location of food
5. Where you'd like your pup during the day and at night (Ex: leave in cage during the day / outside at night).


7. How do I set my schedule?

Click -- HERE -- to go to our scheduling page, then click on the quick booking link. You have the option of creating an account to store your information, and you can select the times that you want your service to take place from there.

Alternatively, you can email (support@pupwalkr.com) or call us at (404-509-6292) if you wish to schedule recurring walks (e.g. 12:00pm - 1:00pm Monday through Friday). Once we have your preferred schedule, we will automatically allocate your walks to these times.


8. How long are the dog walks?

All walks are 30 minutes long.

Set a 1-Hour Range for us to pick up your pup, and we'll get to them within that time. For example, if you schedule a walk and choose a 1-Hour Range from 10:00am - 11:00am, we will pick up your pup and start the walk between 10:00am - 11:00am.


9. What is the 1 Hour Range?

The 1-Hour Range specifies the interval of time that our Walkrs have to pick up the pup. This gives our Walkrs the time they need to properly care for each pup and settle them back in after every walk.

No matter when the Walkr arrives, your pup will always have a full 30 minutes to themselves!


10. What if I have multiple pups?

Our prices will not change based on the number of pups you have, as long as the pups are able to be walked together and can be managed by the dog walkers! This decision will be made by the dog walkers based on your pup's sizes and behavior.

Multiple users may choose to have their walks done together, but will each use a walk credit. Only pups that are owned by a single user can be walked for a single walk credit!

11. Will my pup be walked with others?

No!

We handle every request independently, so your pup(s) will be walked and cared for separately from all other requests.


12. What happens if my pup gets injured or my apartment gets damaged?

All of our dog walks are covered under the PupWalkr Coverage. Should something ever happen to your pup or apartment under our service, our insurance will cover up to $1,000,000 in damages.


13. Can I become a Walkr?

Yes!

We are always on the search for new Walkrs!

Follow our link -- HERE -- to fill out our Walkr form. It'll only take seconds to complete, and we'll have someone reach out to you once it's been submitted!
